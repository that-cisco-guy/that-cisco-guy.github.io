About Jerry.

Jerry is a highly skilled and experienced professional with a background in data systems and network engineering. He served as a data systems technician in the Marine Corps for five years, from 2012 to 2017. After leaving the Marines, he joined Cisco Systems in 2017 as a Network Consulting Engineer, working with several large customers in the route/switch field. In 2020, he began working in network automation and later transition to a Technical Consulting Engineer in the emerging technology field, where he works with cutting-edge technologies like Cisco DNA Center and SD-WAN. Jerry has also undergone extensive training and certifications to become a certified full-stack developer fluent in multiple programming languages, including Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, MySQL, MongoDB, and others. He is also certified as a Cisco DevNet Associate and holds certifications for CompTIA A+, Network +, and Security +.
